A gothic twist on the classic virtual pet genre. Your mystical sphere has needs — hunger, boredom, energy, and hygiene — that you must tend to keep it alive.

**Features:**
- Feed it Spooky Grubs or Fruit Gummies
- Play mini-games like Tic-Tac-Toe and Cat Pong
- Supernatural aesthetic with emoji-based interface
