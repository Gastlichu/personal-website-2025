The Asia section of Disney's Animal Kingdom transports visitors to the foothills of the Himalayas, where the fictional village of Serka Zong serves as base camp for the legendary Expedition Everest.

From weathered prayer flags to intricate temple architecture, every detail captures the spirit of Himalayan culture and the mystique of the world's tallest mountain range.
