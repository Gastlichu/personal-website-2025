Kilimanjaro Safaris takes guests on an 18-minute expedition through the Harambe Wildlife Reserve, a sprawling 110-acre savanna that's home to over 30 species of African wildlife.

From towering giraffes to massive elephants, each safari journey offers unique encounters with animals roaming freely across grasslands, rocky kopjes, and watering holes.
